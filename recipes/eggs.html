Eggs!

Description

Sunny Side Up Eggs! A good solid breakfast to start your day! Comes from chickens! Which are basically mini dinosaurs

Ingredients

  • Two eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  • A Hungry Belly
  • A Pan
  • A Stove
Everything Ready? Ok lets begin!

Steps

  1. Put butter in pan
  2. Heat up pan and stir butter
  3. Crack and pop the two egg yolks in
  4. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper
  5. Wait for the egg to cook till you like it
  6. Enjoy!
