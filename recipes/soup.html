Tomato Soup!

Description

Tomato Soup! Ever feel sick? This delicious meal will take you right back to your sick day from school. Yum yum soup! Add some grilled cheese if youd like as well!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Big Bowl
  • Seasonings
  • A Starving Belly
  • Two Feet
  • A Stove
Everything Ready? Ok lets begin!

Steps

  1. Put tomatoes in bowl
  2. Put bowl on ground
  3. Get to STOMPING
  4. Once properly smushed add some seasonings of whatever you want
  5. Pour bowl contents on stove
  6. Enjoy!
